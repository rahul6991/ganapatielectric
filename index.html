About

Ganapati Electric Co, established in 2007, stands as a beacon of excellence in the electrical distribution industry. Specializing in SWITCHGEAR, MCB’S & DB’S, ELECTRICAL SWITCHES CABLES & WIRES, we have consistently provided custom solutions across various industries, earning a reputation as a market leader.

Our journey began in 2007, and since then, we have grown exponentially by offering innovative products and prompt services to meet the diverse needs of our valued customers. With a legacy of success and a path paved with consistent growth, Ganapati Electric Co has become a trusted name in the electrical materials business since 2007.

Based in Bangalore, we are the premier choice for end-to-end domestic and industrial electrical goods requirements. We are committed to serving as a one-stop solution for all electrical needs, offering the highest quality products and services to our customers and clients.
